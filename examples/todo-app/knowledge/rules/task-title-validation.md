## Statement

A task title must contain between 1 and 200 visible characters after
trimming. Control characters are rejected. Duplicate titles are allowed.

- Applies to: [Todo Item](/concepts/todo-item.md)
- Enforced by: [Todo Service](/components/todo-service.md)
