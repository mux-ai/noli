## Definition

A todo item captures one unit of work: a title, a status, and a priority.
Every todo item belongs to exactly one owner.

## Relationships

- Depends on: [Task Status](/concepts/task-status.md)
- Depends on: [Task Priority](/concepts/task-priority.md)
